Notes¶

Web Application¶

1.0 Carer to view, create and delete patient notes¶

  1. Either click on ‘My Patients’ and select the patient you wish to add a note for or on the patient’s name on the home screen.
  2. Click on ‘Notes’
  1. Enter in the title of the note in the title box and the note in the comment box.
  1. Click submit
  2. All notes can be viewed below and to delete a note click on the red bin and confirming the delete by clicking ‘ok’

2.0 Patients view notes¶

  1. Click on ‘visit notes’ link on the left hand side of the home screen
  1. On this page you can view all the notes your carer has added.

Mobile Application¶

1.0 Carer to view, create and delete patient notes¶

  1. Click on ‘My Patients’ and select the patient you wish to add a note for.
  1. Click on ‘Notes’
  2. Enter in the title of the note in the title box and the note in the comment box.
  1. Click submit
  2. All notes can be viewed below and to delete a note click the delete button.

2.0 Patients view notes¶

  1. On the home page click on the three dots in the top right hand corner
  2. Select ‘Your Notes’
  3. On this page you can view all the notes your carer has added.
