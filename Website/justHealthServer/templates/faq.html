Frequently Asked Questions

  • FAQs

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Please contact us with your question!


  • Who can connect with me?

    • Patient: only carers can search for and connect with you
    Carer: only patients can search for and connect with you


  • Who can see my information?

    • Patient: only you and your carer can see your data
    Carer: your patients can only see your username, firstname and surname


  • Where is my data stored?

    • All your data is stored on the secure JustHealth database. If you would like more information on our security settings, please contact us


  • How can I edit and delete information?

    • Your profile details, appointments and prescriptions can all be edited and deleted by clicking on the item and clicking the appropriate button


  • How can I delete my information from the JustHealth database?

    • To delete all the information that we have stored for you, you need to deactivate your account. Just check the box to remove all your information when you do so


  • How can I leave JustHealth?

    • To deactivate your account, go to Account Settings or Deactivate Account
